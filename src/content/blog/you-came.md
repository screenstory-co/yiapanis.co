---
title: "You Came"
date: "2023-09-23"
excerpt: "An original poem about parenthood, environmental consciousness, and finding roots in the landscape."
---

<p>I never wanted to bring you here.<br/>
This godforsaken place of waste and decay.<br/>
To join this parasitic species we call humanity.<br/>
Eating its host from the inside out.<br/>
In the name of civilisation, progress and modernity.</p>

<p>But you came.<br/>
Like the flood that left behind a discombobulated mess of corpses and concrete.<br/>
This time though, a deluge of love with a pinch of salt.</p>

<p>You were stubborn, like the mangroves which found their roots.<br/>
You helped me find mine, in the wetlands of Yandina Creek.<br/>
And for that, I can rest in peace.</p>
