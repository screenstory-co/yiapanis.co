<p>I had the opportunity to direct a scene under the tutelage of <strong>PJ Hogan</strong> and <strong>Bob Humphreys</strong> as part of my culminating project at Griffith Film School. The exercise involved a &ldquo;mystery&rdquo; script from <em>Proof</em> (1991, written &amp; directed by Jocelyn Moorhouse, starring Hugo Weaving, Genevi&egrave;ve Picot, Russell Crowe). The onus was on the Directors to analyse the screenplay, craft a narrative around it and direct their interpretation.</p>

<p><strong>Genre:</strong> Australian noir</p>
